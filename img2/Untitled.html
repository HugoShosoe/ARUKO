The jade chain lotus, also known as the string tree, is a variety of the jade lotus (Sedum morganianum) also known as the burro's tail or donkey tail. It is a species native to northern Mexico and Honduras. Stone lotus Chain jade is a small herbaceous plant, long stem, few branches but many leaves, stem and leaves are soft, leaves grow densely and stacked in chains. The lotus leaf is bright green, usually 0.5 - 4 cm long, depending on the age of the plant. The flowers are pink, small, growing at the tops of branches or axils of leaves. The type of jade chain with a slow-growing stem, with long pointed leaves pointing upwards is called Chain Jade, and the long, shorter and rounder type is called Chain Jade Bi.
